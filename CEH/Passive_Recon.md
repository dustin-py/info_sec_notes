# **Passive Recon**
---
- Any sort of recon done without actual hacking, such as;
    - *google searches*
    - *social media searches*
    - *freely available info*
    - *job listings*
    - *forums*
    - *non-engaging*
    - *designed to be blind to target*
    - *site clonning*
    - *discover os*
    - *web crawlers/scrappers*

---
